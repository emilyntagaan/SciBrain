Key Concepts

  • **Cell Structure**: Understanding the basic building blocks of life
  • **Organelles**: Specialized structures within cells
  • **Cell Membrane**: The protective barrier that controls what enters and exits the cell

Detailed Explanation

Cells are the fundamental units of life. Every living organism is composed of one or more cells. The cell membrane is a selectively permeable barrier that protects the cell and regulates the passage of materials in and out of the cell.

### Types of Cells

  1. **Prokaryotic Cells**: Simple cells without a nucleus
  2. **Eukaryotic Cells**: Complex cells with a defined nucleus and organelles
